[ About Me ]

From the classroom to the blockchain

I spent 6 years as an English language teacher (2016–2022), getting really good at one thing: making hard things easy to understand. That skill transferred directly into Web3.

Since 2021 I've been creating content across DeFi, NFTs, Layer 1/L2 infrastructure, AI, identity, and privacy. In September 2024 I launched a new YouTube channel. By April 2025 it had 10,000+ subscribers and was monetized. That's 8 months.

I'm a community intern at Billions Network, a brand collaborator for KiiChain and other protocols, and the creator of the Crypto Twitter World Cup. I vibe-code tools, explore ecosystems early, and I believe the best way to grow Web3 is to make it impossible not to understand.
